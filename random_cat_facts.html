Types of Cats

Name Personality Description Picture
Siamese Cat These types of cats are very friendly with humans,
including being very affectionate and showing lots of love.
They are also playful and energetic so you can say they have
the body of a cat but the personality of a dog! They are very good
for a family pet! 		Siamese cats have very light blue eyes. They have pretty long
legs along with a glossy coat. They can come in many different colors
such as blue, lilac, dark brown to black-brown or even warm to medium
brown. Sometimes their coating is darker at some places such as the paws
or ears.
Tabby Cat Tabby Cats are very quiet by mostly taking a nap after
being very active sometimes even for long hours. They are also
very intelligent, for example they are able to understand how
to use a litter box very quickly. These cats are indeed friendly,
many people own these felines and they’re very friendly with their owners. 		Tabby Cats are not actually a type of breed, it's a type of cat
that has any sort of pattern on its coat. So for example it could have
stripes, swirls, spots of different colors on its fur. Also, it’s not
really a specified color, it can be any type. However one thing that all
tabby cats share is an “M” shaped spot on their forehead regardless of
the kind of cat it is.
Persian Cat Persian Cats are pretty quiet cats who just like to
lounge around. They are very affectionate and appreciate
attention from humans; they do not demand/require continuous
attention like dogs or maybe a different type of cat. However
they are relatively calm and preserved cats. 		Persian Cats have a short body with short but thick legs.
The tail and neck are also short but it has small sized ears
with a huge head and round eyes. Most of the common Persian
cats are white, black or blue.
Ragdoll Cats Ragdoll Cats are usually gentle, calm and sociable and
are very affectionate without being demanding. They also love
being carried around a lot. They also require a lot of attention
and you must be with them at most times if wanting to own them. 		Ragdoll Cats usually have a light coloured or cream body coating.
They have short necks and are pretty muscular cats. They are also larger
than most domestic cats as they weigh about 8 - 10 pounds.
Norwegian Forest Cats Norwegian Forest Cats are mostly hunting
types of cats that love to get their prey, however
they also tend to have a gentle nature. Like most
cats this breed is especially independent and does
not cling onto their owners very much. Most of the
time they like to leave their home and explore around.		 Norwegian Forest Cats are pretty big hunters and
are somewhat fast due to their hunter energy. They have
very long and bushy tails and have a very silky coat with
different types of patterns from the colors white, black,
blue and red.
