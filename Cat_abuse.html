Issues of Cat Abuse

Animal abuse is a very serious issue as it is a very inhuman
thing to do considering they are supposed to be our companions.
It happens very often throughout the world, some which are less
severe while others are far worse and more painful and inflicted
upon the animal. Cats are amongst one of the most abused animals as
it is very common to have them as a housepet and they are very
accessible to people.



Abuse can come in many forms, some might be beating or
hitting the animals, while others can be straight up not
feeding and leaving it malnourished for days, weeks or even
months. Being kept in horrible conditions can also be abuse
inflicted upon the animal as it is very unhealthy for them.

An example of this could be showcased in this video:

In this video a woman by the name of Michelline Joy Toulouse was
arrested for 9 counts of animal cruelty after the police found fifty
cats that were malnourished and in filthy conditions at her apartment.
When these cats were found around ten of them were already dead and the
rest were in critical condition. There are many different cases of this
kind, however the scary part is that this woman had a job of being an
animal rescuer. So if the police barely found this person out, how
many more of these situations are continuously happening that are
unknowing to the public?

There are ways we can prevent this!

  • Encourage people to love animals
  • Try to adopt and foster these animals
  • Safely report to the authorities if you witness any animal cruelty
  • Try to advocate and support animal rights organizations!
